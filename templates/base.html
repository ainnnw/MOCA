MO-CA is an application that is here as a solution to overcome this difficulty gap, an application that can be easily accessed by anyone, anytime, anywhere. An application created to help someone to solve the problem of psychological disorders due to suffering from chronic illness. Inside With this application, patients will receive mental support from professional health workers, sharing from families who have treated patients, as well as sharing from patients who have successfully recovered. This application has features in it including group chat, learning about handlers for the psychological impact that arises due to suffering from chronic illness, and the "Support Area" feature in which there is a video to support patients with chronic diseases
